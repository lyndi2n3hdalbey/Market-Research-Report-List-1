<p><p>The CNC automation system market is segmented into several types, including turning, milling, and grinding, each serving distinct manufacturing needs. Turning involves rotating the workpiece against a cutting tool, ideal for cylindrical parts. Milling uses a rotating tool to remove material from a stationary workpiece, suitable for complex shapes. Grinding employs an abrasive wheel to achieve fine surface finishes and precise dimensions. Together, these processes enhance productivity and precision in various industries, driving the CNC market's growth.</p></p>
