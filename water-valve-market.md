<p><p>The water valve market features several key types. Quarter-turn valves enable quick shut-off with a 90-degree turn, making them ideal for on/off applications. Multi-turn valves, requiring multiple rotations to open or close, provide precise flow regulation and are commonly used in throttling services. Control valves, designed for modulating flow rates by varying valve positions, play a crucial role in complex systems requiring accurate flow control. Each type serves distinct functions across various industrial and residential applications.</p></p>
