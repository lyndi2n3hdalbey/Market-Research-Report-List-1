<p><p>The Anti-reflective (AR) and Anti-fingerprint (AF) nanocoating market is categorized primarily into two types based on heating methods: Resistance Heating and Electron Beam Heating. Resistance Heating involves the use of electrical resistance to generate heat, aiding in uniform coating application. In contrast, Electron Beam Heating utilizes high-energy electron beams to precisely vaporize materials for coating, resulting in high-quality finishes. Both methods enhance the performance of coatings, improving optical clarity and surface cleanliness across various applications.</p></p>
