<p><ul><li>Sulfate Process</li><li>Chloride Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rutile Titanium Dioxide market consists of two primary production methods: the sulfate process and the chloride process. The sulfate process involves extracting titanium dioxide from titanium ores using sulfuric acid, resulting in a higher level of impurities, making it suitable for lower-quality applications. In contrast, the chloride process utilizes chlorine to produce high-purity titanium dioxide, offering superior brightness and opacity, making it preferred for high-end products. These processes cater to different market segments based on quality and application needs.</p></p>
