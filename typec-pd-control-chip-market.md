<p><strong>The TypeC PD Control Chip Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UFP</li><li>DFP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Type-C Power Delivery (PD) control chip market is divided into two main types: Upstream Facing Port (UFP) and Downstream Facing Port (DFP). UFP devices, typically found in hosts like laptops and power banks, manage power consumption and negotiate power flow. In contrast, DFP devices, often integrated into peripherals like chargers and docks, are designed to supply power to connected devices. This differentiation allows for optimized power management and enhanced compatibility across various electronic devices and applications.</p></p>
