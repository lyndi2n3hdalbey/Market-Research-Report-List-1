<p><strong>The Medical Balloon System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Quick Exchange Type</li><li>Overall Exchange Type</li><li>Fixed Guide Wire Type</li><li>Perfusion Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical balloon system market comprises several types based on their exchange mechanisms. The Quick Exchange Type allows rapid balloon swapping, enhancing procedural efficiency. The Overall Exchange Type facilitates the simultaneous exchange of guide wires and balloons, suitable for complex interventions. The Fixed Guide Wire Type maintains the wire position during balloon inflation, offering stability. The Perfusion Type integrates continuous fluid delivery through the balloon, allowing for effective drug delivery and improved procedural outcomes during cardiovascular interventions.</p></p>
