<p><ul><li>Seamless Tubing</li><li>Spiral Wound Tubing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Polyimide Tube market comprises two primary types: seamless tubing and spiral wound tubing. Seamless tubing is crafted from a single piece of material, providing superior strength and thermal resistance, ideal for high-performance applications in the aerospace and electronics industries. On the other hand, spiral wound tubing is constructed from multiple layers, offering flexibility and enhanced durability, making it suitable for applications requiring complex shapes and configurations. Both types leverage the unique properties of polyimide for various high-temperature and chemical-resistant uses.</p></p>
