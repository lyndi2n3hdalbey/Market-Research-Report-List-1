<p><p>The agriculture fungicide adjuvants market is categorized into two main types: single-function adjuvants and multifunction adjuvants. Single-function adjuvants are designed to enhance a specific aspect of fungicide performance, such as increasing adherence or improving spread. In contrast, multifunction adjuvants provide multiple benefits, such as enhancing efficacy, improving penetration, and reducing surface tension, thus optimizing the overall performance of fungicides. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing crop protection while minimizing potential negative impacts on the environment.</p></p>
