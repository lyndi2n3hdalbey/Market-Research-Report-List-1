<p><p>The automatic car washer market consists of two primary types: gantry car washes and conveyor tunnel systems. Gantry car washes feature a mobile framework that moves over a stationary vehicle, delivering cleaning solutions and equipment from above. In contrast, conveyor tunnel systems involve vehicles moving through a series of cleaning stations on a conveyor belt, which efficiently applies various cleaning processes. Both systems offer quick and effective services, catering to different customer needs in the growing automated car wash industry.</p></p>
