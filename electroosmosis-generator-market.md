<p><p>The Electroosmosis Generator market is segmented into two main types: Compound Pulse and High Voltage Pulse. Compound Pulse electroosmosis utilizes a series of varying voltage pulses to enhance fluid movement through porous materials, improving efficiency in applications like soil remediation and water treatment. High Voltage Pulse systems, on the other hand, rely on delivering intense, short bursts of electricity to create rapid fluid flow, making them suitable for specialized processes where quick response times are critical. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
