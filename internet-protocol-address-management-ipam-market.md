IPv4, experiencing depletion due to limited address space, often relies on solutions like NAT and subnetting, while IPv6 offers an extensive address space to accommodate the growing number of devices. Effective IPAM solutions help organizations efficiently utilize their IP resources, ensuring seamless connectivity and network management across both protocols.</p></p>
