<p><p>Hormone Pellet Therapy is a treatment method that involves the implantation of small pellets containing bioidentical hormones into the subcutaneous tissue, typically in the hip or buttock area. This therapy is commonly used to address hormonal imbalances associated with menopause, andropause, and other hormonal conditions.</p><p>The Hormone Pellet Therapy Market is experiencing significant growth due to rising awareness of hormone-related health issues and the increasing prevalence of hormonal disorders.
